Gaurang Bansal

Doctrol Student • Security & Privacy, UAVs, Machine Learning, Protocol • National University of Singapore (NUS)

Gaurang Bansal is a President Graduate Fellow and a first-year doctoral student at the National University of Singapore under Prof. Biplab Sikdar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=UR6fy_AAAAAJ&hl=en). He had completed his Master’s and Bachelor’s from BITS Pilani in 2020 & 2018, respectively. His research experience includes mentorship at IIoT lab, BITS Pilani (2017-2020) with Dr. Vinay Chamola.

Currently, he is serving as STEM Ambassador, United Kingdom. He is an active member of IEEE Sensors Council, IEEE Systems Council, ACM Student Chapter and Eta Kappa Nu society. His research interests includes security & privacy in Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) networks using Cryptography, Deep Learning and DLT’s. He has authored works in top-tier conferences and journals such as IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology, IEEE INFOCOM, IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering, etc. He is also an invited reviewer for IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology, IEEE Globecom, IET Image Processing, Elsevier FGCS Journal.
