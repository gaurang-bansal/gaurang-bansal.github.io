Gaurang Bansal is doctoral student and recipient of the President Graduate Fellowship at the National University of Singapore (NUS) under [Prof. Biplab Sikdar](https://www.ece.nus.edu.sg/stfpage/bsikdar/) at [Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering](https://www.eng.nus.edu.sg/ece/).  Previously, he had completed his Master's and Bachelor's from BITS Pilani in 2020 & 2018, respectively. Currently, he is also serving as Web Editor for [ACM XRDS Magazine](https://xrds.acm.org/).  

His research interests include wireless IoT, network security, queuing theory, blockchain and machine learning. His research has been funded by the ASEAN-India S&T Development Fund (AISTDF), DST and National University of Singapore.

Gaurang has received "Best Scholar Award" by [International Research Awards on Science, Health and Engineering](https://shen.sciencefather.com/view/award-winners) and [Distinguished Scientist Fellow](https://dsfp.ksu.edu.sa/en/content/about-us) nomination from King Saud University. He also serves as a member of [Eta Kappa Nu](https://hkn.ieee.org/) and [Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF)](https://www.ietf.org/). He has also served as co-organiser and TPC chair for various reputed workshops like [IEEE Globecom](https://globecom2021.ieee-globecom.org/workshop/ws-10-workshop-ai-and-blockchain-enabled-secure-and-privacy-preserving-air-and-ground-smart) workshop and [IEEE INFOCOM](https://infocom2022.ieee-infocom.org/artificial-intelligence-ai-and-blockchain-enabled-secure-and-privacy-preserving-air-and-ground-smart) workshop.
